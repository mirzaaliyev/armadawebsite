Hennessy

The Hennessy cognac distillery was founded by Richard Hennessy, an Irish officer serving in the army of Louis XV.The Richard Hennesy, Irish captain went to heal Wounds to France, to the garrison at island of Re. Enchanted by beauty river Charentes and the magnificence boundless vineyards, he has left his military service and settled in the city of Cognac, where founded the Hennessy Cognac House.
